About Mukund Joshi!

Mukund Joshi, is India's very renowned AAC Consultant and Business Coach for AAC Plant Owners and CEO's. He is a seasoned Concrete Technologist and AAC (Autoclaved Aerated Concrete) Specialist by profession.

His journey in the world of concrete and AAC spans over three decades, during which he has amassed a wealth of knowledge and experience which he is passionate about sharing with others. Following his passion, Mukund Joshi has developed a very unique and exclusive training program for AAC Block Manufacturers in India to set-up and scale their manufacturing plants from zero.

He is an Author of Best Selling AAC Book

Mastering AAC Blocks

Master the Art and Science of AAC Technology

I am also proud to be the author of the Amazon best-seller "MASTERING AAC BLOCKS." This book encapsulates my extensive knowledge and insights into AAC technology, providing readers with a comprehensive guide to mastering the production and application of AAC blocks.
