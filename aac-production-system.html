How does AAC Production System Works?


Book a Consultation

Implementation

It is a process control system that is setup on the shop floor in your plant.

Monitoring

Under this Production System, 75 critical parameters are monitored every day in the prodcution process.

Our Auditor visits every 10 days to the plant - 3 Days a months - to monitor the process.

Staff Engagement

Each staff member become clear about his roles and responsibilities under this system.

Gamification

Each team member is given a score card and they score points for the completed tasks. Each team member gets the monitory awards for the points they won.

Performance Review

Every month management can review under performing teams, understand their challenges, give additional support when required, or take actions against prennial under performers.
